Problem

People want to share files with there friends and colleagues, all the time. But it is difficult to send the files using the simple email services as these service puts on the limit on the amount and type of the data to be transferred. We at webTransfer are trying to solve this problem.

At webTransfer, we try to make it easier for the people to share files and other stuff with there colleagues in a fast and efficient way!
